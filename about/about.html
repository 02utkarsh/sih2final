About Us

This is about making higher education in India more environmentally and socially responsible. It aims to create a unified platform for planning, executing, and monitoring sustainability initiatives in colleges and universities. These initiatives include using cleaner energy sources, building eco-friendly campuses, and following green construction standards. The goal is to involve all stakeholders, including students and administrators, in this effort. By doing so, the solution aims to produce a generation of environmentally conscious citizens and align with India's commitments under the Paris Agreement. It also seeks to learn from global best practices and use data-driven dashboards to track progress effectively.
